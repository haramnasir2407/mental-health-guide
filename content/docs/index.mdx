## Why Mental Wellbeing at Work Matters

Mental wellbeing is a crucial predictor of general wellbeing and directly impacts workplace efficacy, motivation and job satisfaction levels.

- 43% of people from around 100 countries have experienced workplace burnout.
- 1 in 6 people experience mental health problems in the workplace
- 82% of employees in the tech industry feel close to burnout
- 12 billion working days are lost every year to depression and anxiety

## What You Will Find in This Playbook

- Deeper insight into the impact of mental health on workplace productivity and developing harmonious work-life integration
- Measures for assessing mental health challenges and gaining greater emotional self-awareness
- Empirically proven strategies, tools and resources for contending with mental health challenges
- Strategies for fostering workplace resilience and enabling optimum performance
