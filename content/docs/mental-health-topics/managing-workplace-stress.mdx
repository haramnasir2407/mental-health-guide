title: Managing Workplace Stress
---

Workplace stress is one of the leading factors affecting employee mental wellbeing. While mild stress may motivate us, excessive and prolonged stress can lead to burnout - characterized by energy depletion, increased mental distance from work, and ineffectiveness.

## Identifying Signs and Symptoms of Workplace Stress

Recognising the various signs and indications of stress and burnout in the work space, is crucial for implementing effective coping strategies. Stress may pose emotional challenges and alter our behaviours and thinking patterns, thereby leading towards a decline in productivity and quality of work.
